Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Community Impact

Shanti Yuwa Club was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate young individuals who wanted to make a positive impact in their community. The name "Shanti Yuwa" translates to "Peace Youth" in English, reflecting our commitment to promoting peace, harmony, and positive social change through youth-led initiatives.

Since our inception, we have been dedicated to addressing various social issues in Biratnagar and surrounding areas through community development projects, awareness campaigns, educational initiatives, and environmental conservation efforts.

Our organization is entirely youth-led, with members ranging from high school students to young professionals united by their desire to create a better society and empower other youth to become agents of change.

Our Mission

To empower youth to become catalysts for positive social change by providing them with the platforms, resources, and skills needed to address community challenges and foster sustainable development.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Our core values shape everything we do and define who we are as an organization.

Compassion

We approach all our work with empathy and understanding, recognizing the diverse needs and challenges within our community.

Inclusivity

We believe in creating equal opportunities for all, regardless of gender, religion, caste, ethnicity, or socioeconomic background.

Sustainability

We are committed to implementing programs and initiatives that have lasting impact and can be sustained by the community in the long term.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and novel approaches to address social issues and community needs.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and working together with other organizations, government bodies, and community stakeholders.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our activities and operations.
